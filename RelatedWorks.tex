\section{Related Work}
In order to determine the scope of our work, we conducted a literature review on decision trees and classification problems. Pandey 2013 outlined a few attributes to take note of when training a decision tree, as well as a variety of decision trees models suitable for construction. Their research found that models of decision trees called J48 and C 4.5 are suitable for a prediction of grades of students. This is further accentuated by Bujang et al. 2021, which outlined a multi-class decision tree-based model (more specifically J48).

Most importantly, Ramirez 2023 delineated the procedure to train a decision tree for a classification for transport systems. We thus utilize their research as our baseline for our methodologies. Kuzilek 2017 provided the dataset regarding academic performances, which we shall use to train the policy set of our decision tree.
