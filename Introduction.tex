\section{Introduction}
Higher education institutions (HEIs) have been of increasing importance in recent years, given their role in developing a more sustainable society. It stands to reason that preventing dropout and ensuring students' receive proper support is crucial. Various frameworks have been proposed to accurately gauge the degree of supports a student demand \cite{Haddou2025,Stoiber2015}.

Of our particular interest is the Multi-Tier Systems of Support (MTSS) framework.
Using a given dataset of students' data and a multitude of prompting techniques, we aim to identify the benefits of the manner in which we prompt Large Language Models (LLM) to give an MTSS assistance type based on a student's data.
